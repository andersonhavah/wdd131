Find Authentic Flavors

Ready to explore Togolese cuisine in person? Here are some tips for finding great local food, especially around Lomé.

Tips for Finding Local Food

  • Visit the Markets: Places like the Grand Marché in Lomé offer a vibrant atmosphere and numerous food stalls selling snacks and simple meals.
  • Look for 'Maquis': These are often open-air, informal restaurants or bars serving popular local dishes like grilled fish, chicken, and fufu at reasonable prices.
  • Try Street Food (with care): Togo has delicious street food like grilled corn, fried yams ('ignames frites'), doughnuts ('beignets'), and meat skewers ('brochettes'). Choose vendors that look clean and popular.
  • Ask Locals: Don't hesitate to ask hotel staff, taxi drivers, or friendly locals for their recommendations on where to eat authentic Togolese food.
  • Be Adventurous: Step outside the main tourist areas and explore different neighborhoods – you might find hidden culinary gems!
