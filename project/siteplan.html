Taste of Togo Site Plan

Anderson Havah

Overview

Site Name: TASTE OF TOGO

The name is evocative, suggesting a sensory experience related to the website's topic and serving as a resource to explore Togolese food, its traditions. "Togo" clearly identifies the geographical focus. It's memorable and relevant.

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to serve as an introductory guide to the diverse and flavorful cuisine of Togo for an international and local audience. The site will provide information on popular Togolese dishes, staple ingredients, and cultural context related to food in Togo. It will also offer tips on finding authentic Togolese food, particularly in Lomé. The site will be visually appealing and user-friendly, with a focus on accessibility and responsiveness.

Audience

Tourists planning a visit to Togo, food enthusiasts interested in West African cuisine, members of the Togolese diaspora looking for familiar flavors, students, and locals seeking information or simple recipe ideas.

Scenarios

I'm visiting Lomé next month and I'm excited to try the local food, but I feel overwhelmed. What are some essential Togolese dishes I absolutely must try, and what are they made of?

I'm a Togolese student studying abroad, and I miss the flavors of home. Can you recommend some easy-to-make Togolese recipes that I can prepare in my dorm?

I'm a food enthusiast who loves exploring different cuisines. What are some unique ingredients and cooking techniques used in Togolese cuisine that I should know about?

I'm a tourist in Lomé, and I want to experience authentic Togolese food. Where can I find local restaurants or markets that offer traditional dishes?

"I know Fufu is common, but I've heard there are dierent types and sauces. Can you explain more about Fufu and what typically accompanies it?

Content

Home page

Header: Site Name/Logo, Navigation (Home, Dishes, Find Food, References)

Main: Welcome message, brief intro to Togolese cuisine's appeal. Engaging banner image. "Dish of the Week" section (JS-powered: image, name, brief description). Overview of key staple ingredients (list format).

Footer: Contact info, social media links, copyright notice (dynamic year via JS).

Dishes Page

Header: Consistent with Home
Main: Brief intro text. Filter controls (e.g., dropdown or buons for main ingredient - JS powered). Grid/list area where dish cards are dynamically generated by JS (each card: image, name, short description).

Footer: Consistent with Home

Find Food Page

Header: Consistent with Home

Main: Title ("Find Authentic Flavors"). Section with tips for finding authentic food (markets, types of eateries). User Submission Form (fields: Name [optional], Location/Restaurant Name, Dish Recommendation, Comments; Submit buon). JS will be used for form interaction/validation and potentially saving data to `localStorage` (as per project requirements).
