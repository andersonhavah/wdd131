Key Ingredients

Togolese cooking relies on fresh, local ingredients. Some common staples include:

  • Maize (Corn)
  • Yams
  • Cassava
  • Beans
  • Okra
  • Peanuts (Groundnuts)
  • Palm Nuts
  • Chillies & Spices
